Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are the most common questions we receive from potential clients.

How long does a typical project take?

Most websites take 4-8 weeks from start to finish, depending on complexity and content readiness. CRM setup usually takes 2-3 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our discovery call.

What's included in your web design service?

Everything you need: custom design, mobile optimization, SEO foundation, content management system, contact forms, analytics setup, and 30 days of post-launch support.

Do you work with businesses outside New Zealand?

Absolutely! While we're based in Wellington, we work with clients globally. All our communication and project management processes are designed for remote collaboration.

What happens after my website launches?

You get 30 days of complimentary support, plus lifetime technical guidance. We also offer ongoing maintenance packages and can help with future updates and improvements.

Can you help improve my existing website?

Yes! We offer website redesigns, performance optimization, and feature additions. We'll assess your current site and recommend the best path forward during our consultation.

What makes WingfieldGemini different?

You work directly with the founder, not a junior developer. We focus on results, not just pretty designs. Every project is custom-built, and we're committed to your long-term success.
